How pricing works here

No comp feed? Then no fake ARV. You get a sale band instead.

Broward publishes no live comp feed, so the Deal Analyzer will not print a per-property ARV here — that would be a guess dressed up as a number. Instead, every Broward lead carries a ZIP-level $/sf band built from qualified arm’s-length single-family sales (2024 onward) on the state roll: the median, 25th and 75th percentile $/sf, with the sale count shown. ZIPs with fewer than 8 usable sales are omitted rather than padded.
