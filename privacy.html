Privacy Policy.

Last updated · July 2026

Maco Equity Partners ("we", "us") operates macoequitypartners.com. We collect as little personal information as this site can function with, and we do not sell personal information to anyone.

What we collect

  • Contact & enquiry forms. If you submit our enquiry or research-subscription form, we receive the information you type (such as your name and email address). Form submissions are delivered to us by FormSubmit, a third-party form relay.
  • Analytics. We use Plausible Analytics, a privacy-focused, cookie-free analytics service. It reports aggregate page statistics and does not build visitor profiles or track you across other sites.
  • No advertising trackers. This site sets no advertising cookies and runs no ad-network code.

How we use it

Contact details are used solely to respond to your enquiry or to send you the research you asked for. You can unsubscribe or ask us to delete your details at any time by emailing rmac@macoequitypartners.com.

Third-party services

Pages on this site load resources from third parties that have their own privacy practices: Google Fonts (typefaces), jsDelivr/cdnjs (open-source libraries), OpenFreeMap (map tiles on our homepage), Plausible (analytics), and FormSubmit (form delivery). None of these are provided your form contents except FormSubmit, which relays them to us.

The investor portal

Our deal-analysis portal stores its working data (pipeline notes, settings) in your own browser's local storage. That data stays on your device; it is not transmitted to us.

Questions

Email rmac@macoequitypartners.com with any privacy question or request.
