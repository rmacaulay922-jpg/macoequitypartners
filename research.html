Methodology & Sources

Market data is sourced from CoStar, Colliers South Florida, JLL Research, CBRE Econometric Advisors, Real Capital Analytics, Dodge Data & Analytics, and public county records. Vacancy rates are calculated on a direct basis excluding sublease space. Rental rates represent triple-net (NNN) asking rents. Transaction data reflects arms-length sales recorded during the reporting period. Construction pipeline data reflects ground-up industrial projects tracked by CoStar and Dodge Data & Analytics. Pre-lease rates are based on signed leases as of March 31, 2026.
