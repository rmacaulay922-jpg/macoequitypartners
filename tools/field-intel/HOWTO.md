# Field Intel Memo — monthly workflow (60-90 min)

The Principal-tier deliverable. One memo a month, 3-5 walked properties.
This is the one thing PropStream structurally cannot copy — an operator's eyes.

## The loop

1. **Pick (10 min).** Open the portal → Top 50 Deals → sort "Best buy grade".
   Pick 3-5 you haven't walked, ideally clustered so it's one drive.
2. **Drive (40-60 min).** From the public right-of-way only. Per property note
   4-6 concrete things: roof (tarps? sag? age guess), windows (original/impact),
   yard/driveway (occupancy tells), meter, door notices, the NEIGHBORS, street.
   Voice-memo it while parked — specifics beat adjectives.
3. **Write (15 min).** Copy `TEMPLATE.html` to `2026-08.html` (never commit dated
   memos — they're paid content; the folder's dated files are gitignored). Paste a
   deal block per property, fill from your notes — or hand Claude the voice-memo
   transcript and the addresses and ask it to fill the template in your voice.
4. **Send.** Open the file in a browser → print to PDF → email to your DA-SUB
   list with two lines of intro. Done.

## Rules that keep it valuable

- The verdict line is the product: PURSUE / PASS / WATCH with the honest number.
  A memo that passes on everything is MORE credible, not less.
- Never publish full addresses beyond your subscribers; memos use the same
  "148··" masking as the site if a memo ever leaves the list.
- Re-check the engine's numbers the morning you send (scores move with the feeds).
