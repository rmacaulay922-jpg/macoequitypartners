# Week 1: Ten Customers — The Playbook

Goal: **10 paying subscribers ($500 MRR) in 7 days.** Honest funnel math with no track
record: figure ~60–80 real conversations → 20–25 activated trials → 10 paid. That means
this is a *conversations* game, not a posting game. Every day has a number.

**Definition of "secured":** card charged, or a written "yes, bill me" with a payment
link sent. Trials count as pipeline, not customers.

## Day 1–2 — the warm 20 (target: 4 paid)

List the 20 people who already know you move deals: brokers who've sent you pocket
listings, your hard-money lender + their other borrowers, contractors, the title rep,
past partners, REIA people you text. Personal message, not broadcast:

> "I built the tool I use to find my own off-market deals — it reads Dade/Broward
> county records (code cases, liens, estates) and ranks everything, with honest
> underwriting. I'm opening 25 founding spots at $50/mo locked. Want a week free to
> try it? No card."

Warm intros close at 20–30%. Twenty asks ≈ 4–6 customers. **This is the highest-yield
hour of the week; do it before any public posting.**
