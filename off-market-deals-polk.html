What the Polk data includes — and what it doesn’t.

Polk sits in the “Live · expanding” tier, and it is the biggest of the band counties: 867 leads, sourced from the county’s own Property Appraiser service rather than the state roll — which is why every lead here carries full sale history.

Not here — on purpose

  • No per-property ARV. Polk publishes no open comp feed, and we won’t print a number a feed can’t back

How pricing works here

No comp feed? Then no fake ARV. You get a sale band instead.

Polk publishes no live comp feed, so the Deal Analyzer will not print a per-property ARV here — that would be a guess dressed up as a number. Instead, every Polk lead carries a ZIP-level $/sf band built from qualified arm’s-length single-family sales (2024 onward) on the state roll: the median, 25th and 75th percentile $/sf, with the sale count shown. ZIPs with fewer than 8 usable sales are omitted rather than padded.
